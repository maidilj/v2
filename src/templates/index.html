About

From Materials
to Measurement

I am a Singaporean research scientist with a background spanning thermo-electrical simulation, instrumental expertise, sensors and data-driven method development in both academic and industry settings.

At P&G and HP-NTU, I built investigative systems to decode real-world behaviour — designing and deploying sensor instruments in field conditions, stress-testing them against failure modes, and translating raw multi-modal data into interpretable simulation & models and defensible insights that shaped product and design decisions.

I am now seeking to apply this foundation to forensic physics and engineering, with a particular interest in fire science, combustion dynamics, and evidence-based investigative methods.
